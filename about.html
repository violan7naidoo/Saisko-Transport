About Us

Saisko Transport was established in 2003. For the past 18 years we have been committed to provide high quality transportation services to our clients on a continuous basis. We offer transport for both local and long distance to anywhere in South Africa. We provide efficient and reliable delivery services with the best value to clients, which is what makes our existing customers to feel that we are a part of their business.


Mission Statement

We are committed to make continuous improvements that establish quality services in an excellent working environment that meets the expectations of our customers. Saisko Transport has always provided reliable, safe and cost effective service throughout the years and we will continue to do so.
